It was during the summer of 2020 when I decided to follow my lifelong dream of studying journalism. I was accepted to Algonquin College for their diploma program and after graduating I continued to the University of Ottawa to get my undergrad. The experience has been overwhelming. The journalism program at both AC and uOttawa have given me the opportunity to meet friends and talk to people about what is going on for them in the community. I wish I could go back in time to tell myself to buckle in because the experience is going to be hectic, but worth every moment of it.

Experience

  • Algonquin Times: I was in school at Algonquin College I wrote a few stories as part of my assignments. These articles were published on the school’s news website!
  • Kitchissippi Times: I’ve had the opportunity to dabble in freelancing and submitted two stories to the Kitchissippi Times. It’s been an enjoyable experience writing for a community paper and hope to continue contributing.
